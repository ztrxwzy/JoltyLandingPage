About Us

Jolty is an online digital distribution platform designed to boost the success of independent developers and studios in the video game industry.

Our focus is to provide a flexible, transparent, fair and equitable platform that provides visibility and monetization opportunities for game creators.

At Jolty, we believe in the power of the community and the unlimited potential of every creator we will forge a future where all talents are recognized and rewarded as they deserve.
